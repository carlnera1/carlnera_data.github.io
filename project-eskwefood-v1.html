EskweFood - Improving Customer Experience for Increased Profitability & Retention

The primary goal of this group project was to develop data-backed recommendations to increase profitability and customer retention for the food delivery platform, EskweFood.

Created with Eskwelabs Group Team

Skills utilized: Python, Data Analysis, Pandas, Numpy, Data Cleaning

The Project Scenario

This study investigates the operational and customer behavior data of the EskweFood delivery platform to identify key drivers of profitability and customer retention. By analyzing delivery metrics, spending habits, and the impact of discounts, the project proposes data-driven strategies to shift from margin-eroding promotions toward a reliability-focused convenience membership.
